In an increasingly digital age, exhibitions are the only media where buyers, sellers and products physically come together - a potent force for business. Exhibitions are one of the most effective media for establishing and maintaining customer relations. Exhibitions becomes a success only if it is planned and organized properly by taking into account the position of the stalls, the total ambience of the hall etc. .
