ENTERTAINMENT & SHOW MANAGEMENT

Entertainment is for amusement. After a day of tedious work, people would love to attend shows that would relax their mind. Entertainment and show management has gained popularity in the recent years. We have the expertise and contacts for arranging and coordinating stage shows, live bands, theatres, dance shows and other cultural programmes. Whether it is inviting a celebrity for a stage performance or arranging a traditional stage show, we are the “people” to make it happen.

    Our Entertainment and Show Management includes

  • Social and Cultural Programmes
  • Traditional Reception and Cultural Shows
  • Celebrity Shows
  • Ethnic Art and Dance Shows
  • Fusion of Dance or Music
  • Classical and Modern Dance Forms

Fashion Shows

Performances on stage, for fashion shows, plays, etc. require special ambience, attention to detail and above all a certain taste. R.I.O.T Events will help you whip up all of the above at one go that leave the audience enchanted .
